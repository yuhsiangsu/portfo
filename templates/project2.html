Overview

In this project, we design the control structure and tune the control parameters for the given autonomous buggy to achieve the line following function and compete in the assigned path.The project was awarded second place in the Autonomous Buggy Control 2019 by Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, The University of Manchester.

Control Structure

The control structure consists of two loops: (a)inner loop and (b) outer loop. The inner loop is the PI contorller for each of the two motors and the other loop is a bang-bang controller based on the feedback measurement of the reflectance sensor.
